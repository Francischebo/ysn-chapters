Overview

The Youth Synergy Network (YSN) Leadership Structure is dynamic, multi-tiered, and youth-driven. It is designed to empower decision-making from the grassroots to the national level, promoting accountability, inclusion, and innovation across all regions and campuses.

YSN Leadership Structures

The organizational framework that ensures effective governance and operations

National Secretariat

A professionally organized body based in Nairobi and housed within partner institutions or hosted by supporting law faculties. The Secretariat performs the following roles:

  • Policy development and strategic planning
  • Branding, communications, and media coordination
  • Fundraising and partnership engagement
  • Data collection and program evaluation
  • Member welfare and mental health support coordination
  • Legal research and documentation

Staff includes:

  • Programs Officer
  • Communications & Design Officer
  • Mental Health Coordinator
  • Volunteer Manager
  • Legal & Policy Associate

University Chapter Leadership

Each of the 15 university chapters operates under a Chapter Executive Committee, ensuring autonomy in local programming while aligning with national objectives.

The chapter leaders:

  • Plan and execute campus-based projects
  • Report monthly to the Regional Coordinator
  • Participate in annual strategic summits
  • Nominate representatives to the National Youth Assembly

The National Youth Assembly (NYA)

YSN's democratic youth decision-making body. Comprises:

  • Two elected representatives from each university chapter
  • Members of the National Secretariat
  • Ex-officio founders and advisors

Mandate:

  • Debates policy proposals
  • Approves annual calendar of events
  • Reviews constitutional changes
  • Passes motions on urgent youth matters
  • Nominates representatives for regional or international conferences

The NYA meets biannually, with additional virtual sessions as needed.

Alumni Council

Former chapter leaders and national officers are enrolled in the Youth Synergy Alumni Council, which:

  • Offers mentorship to current student leaders
  • Provides career guidance and legal placement support
  • Supports fundraising and long-term visioning
  • Promotes intergenerational knowledge transfer

Alumni are featured in the "Where Are They Now?" series in the News & Media section.
